Physical and Mental Health Among Students

By: Vivian Nguyen, Rohan Makkar, Vanessa Shimizu

Introduction:

Our mental health includes the way we act, feel, and think. Many students suffer from poor mental health due to the stress they face from school. Peer pressure to be successful often cause us to dive too deep into our studies. In turn, this not only impacts our mental health, but our physical being as well. To prevent this, students should exercise, meditate, and have a healthy sleep schedule.

Data on Students' Mental Health:

From 2016-2019, children aged 3-17 have been diagnosed with ADHD, anxiety, behavior problems, and depression. And each year, the rates increase.
- 9.8% have ADHD
- 9.4% have anxiety
- 8.9% have behavior problems
- 4.4% have depression
